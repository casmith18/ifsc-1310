    • Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    Progressive enhancement can be considered one of the most important things in web design. Progressive enhancement allows the creator of a specific website to focus more on the actual content rather than making the content for a specific engine or browser. Progressive enhancement allows everyone the basics of the content regardless of what browser or type of internet they are using. This is incredibly important because it allows more people to connect to the creators content regardless of their hardware or software.
